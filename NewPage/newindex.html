About Us

Limiting Factor is a student-founded and student-run fictional company established in 2025. Our mission is to empower individuals to set and achieve their own limits through innovative solutions and a supportive community. We believe that everyone has the potential to push beyond their boundaries and reach new heights. Join us on this journey of self-discovery and growth as we explore the limitless possibilities together.
